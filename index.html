In response to the growing demand for quality English-medium education in Chittagong and the lack of suitable institutions, a group of education enthusiasts initiated a project in 2012 to establish an English Medium School and College following the national curriculum. The key motivations were: providing students with a competitive edge for higher education and job opportunities, fostering a sense of national culture and identity, and promoting moral and religious values amidst their decline.
